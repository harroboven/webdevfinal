About me

In the summer of 2015 I was in Barcelona and I felt like playing tennis. This turned out to be really difficult to arrange. I thought: “You have Booking.com for hotels. But why is there no Booking.com for sport locations?” The idea would not let me go, so in the fall of 2016 I co-founded Movit Sport. During my startup journey I learned the hard way that I needed technical skills that I didn’t have.

I hope I’ll be able to apply the knowledge and skills I acquired at the Web Development Bootcamp to build with my co-founder a gorgeous version 3.0 of our platform that will allow us to scale. This is a first prototype that hopefully will get us closer to that goal.
